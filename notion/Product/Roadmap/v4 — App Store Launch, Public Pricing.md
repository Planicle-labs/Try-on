# v4 — App Store Launch, Public Pricing, First 10 Paying Merchants

**Timeframe: 2 to 3 weeksGoal: Cross the threshold from “beta product” to “public product with paying customers”**

### What to build

**App Store Listing:**
- Title: specific and benefit-led (“Virtual Try-On + Size Recommendation for Clothing Stores”)
- Subtitle: the one-line value prop (“Reduce returns. Increase conversion. Let shoppers see how clothes fit before they buy.”)
- Description: lead with outcomes (return rate, conversion), not features (AI, Gemini). Merchants buy outcomes.
- Screenshots: show the storefront try-on flow AND the merchant dashboard. Both audiences matter.
- Demo video: 60-90 seconds maximum. Show the consumer try-on in 30 seconds. Show the dashboard in 30 seconds. End with a merchant testimonial quote if you have one.
- Key features section: 5 bullet points maximum. Each one is a merchant benefit, not a technology description.

**Launch Sequence:**
- Week 1 after submission: while waiting for review, build your distribution list
- Post in r/shopify, Shopify Partner Slack, Indian founder communities (Entrackr, StartupIndia groups)
- Reach out to 20 Indian D2C clothing brands directly (LinkedIn, cold email)
- Write one detailed post on your build journey for an audience of Shopify merchants
- Week 2: App Store approval (optimistically). Flip from private to public listing.
- Week 3: First paid conversions. Target 10 paying merchants by end of week 3.

**Support infrastructure:**
- Install Crisp or Intercom in the merchant admin panel (free tiers are fine to start)
- Create 5 help articles: getting started, adding size charts, understanding the dashboard, billing FAQ, photo quality guide for merchants
- Set up a status page (free with BetterUptime or similar) so merchants can check if there are outages

**Metrics to track from day 1 of public launch:**
- Daily installs and uninstalls (track net install rate, not just gross installs)
- Time-to-first-generation (your north star onboarding metric)
- Free-to-paid conversion rate (target: 15% within 14 days)
- Generation volume per merchant per day (tells you engagement depth)
- Churn rate after first billing cycle (this is the metric that tells you if you have real product-market fit)

### v3 success criteria

- [ ]  App Store listing live and publicly searchable
- [ ]  10 paying merchants (any paid tier) within 4 weeks of launch
- [ ]  Average time-to-first-generation under 20 minutes
- [ ]  Zero critical production incidents in first 2 weeks
- [ ]  First merchant case study written (even one data point: “X merchant saw Y% reduction in returns”)
- [ ]  Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) positive — even ₹20,000 MRR is a proof point
